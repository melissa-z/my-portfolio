VibeCheck

Promote party safety on Brown's campus and beyond, by providing a way for students to report assault and unacceptable conduct at events in a simple, accessible, and anonymous way.

My Role:

Full-stack Developer
UI/UX Designer

Team:

3 Developers

Tools:

Figma, React Native, MariaDB

Challenge

Students often feel unsafe at parties, but are too intimidated to report unacceptable conduct in formal ways. Hosting organizations such as fraternities and sports houses are not held responsible for their behavior.

Solution

VibeCheck provides students with a simple, accessible way of anonymously reporting unacceptable behavior, and builds a public database of party safety feedback. The app targets organizations such as fraternities and sports houses, and incentivize hosts to promote safe conduct at their events.

Welcome

To ensure full anonymity, the only user information recorded by VibeCheck is gender and class year to promote user trust.

Rate Event

A simple 3-card rating system is optimized for easy access and use while attending events, even when users are intoxicated.

View Stats

Users can review an organization's safety feedback over time, before choosing to attend a party.

Detailed Evaluation

Users also have the option to submit more comprehensize feedback for an organization. Responses in detailed evaluations are reviewed by a third party health organization at Brown, such as BWell.

Reflection

  • Development for this project is still ongoing, but I've already learned a lot!
  • Designing for potentially intoxicated users pushed us to simplify designs as much as possible, which I think ended up improving the product.
  • Anonymity and information privacy were surprisingly difficult considerations to handle. We came across many ideas such as adding maps, real-time feedback, and public comments, which we then realized could reveal dangerous information if accessed by people outside of the Brown community.
  • I helped to design the database schemas for the project, and learned to use relational databases and MySQL!
